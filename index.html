About

Being born and raised in Southern California, I grew up with a passion of sketching and entering art contests. For my senior year in high school, I wanted a challenging course, so I enrolled in the Pascal programming class. With a Bachelor’s in Electronics Engineering, I gained experience repairing several types of electronic products. With a growing passion for learning, I achieved a goal of obtaining a Master’s in Business Administration degree. My most recent career was as a Logistics Manager, in the pro audio and lighting industry. In this role, I helped improved day-to-day operations and the company’s CRM.

It took me a year of considering a search for a new career. With my experience of researching topics, through the MBA program, web development peaked my interest. After researching several bootcamps in the area, LearningFuze’s alumni reviews of the instructors and experiences helped me understand this was the right place to be.

During my time at LearningFuze, I have been coding 12 hours a day. LearningFuze has helped me with team development and confidence in my coding abilities. I learned HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, React, JavaScript, Ajax. I developed several apps such as Guessing Game and Memory Match Game. For the team hackathons, we collaborated on an Astrology Word Search Game. I see myself working at a company, where I can learn and improve my creativity.

To sum everything about me: I’m a thinker, I’m a doer, I’m a snowboarder, I’m a portrait artist, I’m an entrepreneur, I’m a foodie, I’m an event promoter and producer, I’m a nightlife socialite, and I’m a developer.
