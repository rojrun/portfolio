About

Being born and raised in Southern California, I grew up with a passion for sketching and entering art contests, which later evolved to drawing portraits. For my senior year in high school, I wanted a challenging course, so I enrolled in the Pascal programming class. After high school, I got involved in event production, those were fun times. For 5 years, I learned about event promotion, event planning, event production, decorating, and social networking. With a Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering, I gained experience repairing several types of products, for 7 years. With a growing passion for learning, I achieved a goal of obtaining a Master of Business Administration degree. That lead me to an 8-year logistics management career, in the pro audio and lighting industry. In this role, I helped improved day-to-day operations and the company’s CRM.

It was time for another career change, the software industry. With my experience of researching topics, through the MBA program, web development peaked my interest. I attended LearningFuze, a coding bootcamp, which has helped me with team development and confidence in my coding abilities.

To sum everything about me: I’m a thinker, I’m a doer, I'm a weightlifter, I’m a snowboarder, I’m a portrait artist, I’m an entrepreneur, I’m a foodie, I’m an event promoter and producer, I’m a reveller, and I’m a developer.

So, I decided to freelance as a new career, which led you to me.
